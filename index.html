About ShramSetu

ShramSetu was founded in 2020 with a vision to transform the unorganized labor market in India. We believe that every skilled worker deserves fair opportunities and recognition for their craftsmanship.

Our platform bridges the gap between skilled workers and customers, creating a win-win situation for both parties. Workers get consistent employment opportunities, while customers get reliable, verified professionals for their needs.

Our Mission

To empower skilled workers by providing them with a platform that recognizes their talent, ensures fair compensation, and helps them grow professionally.

Our Vision

To create an ecosystem where skilled workers are respected, valued, and have access to continuous growth opportunities.
